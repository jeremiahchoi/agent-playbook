# 0000 — Short decision title (client)

- **Status:** accepted | superseded by NNNN
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** who approved (client side + our side)
- **Source:** where it was decided — e.g. J. Smith email, YYYY-MM-DD, "Re: subject" / call notes link

## Context

2–4 sentences: what prompted this. What was the client reacting to?

## Decision

1–3 sentences, imperative: what was agreed.

## Consequences

- What changes for the engagement (scope, timeline, deliverables)
- What we're giving up / risks accepted
- Follow-ups this creates (→ tracker, not here)
